How to compile: gcc -o field field.c -pthread

The program simulates a shared field by 3 sports: football, baseball, and
rugby. All 3 sports will have access to the field at different times.
Football will play games with 22 people on the field at once while baseball
will play games with 18 people. Rugby can play with any number of players between 2 and 30 .
